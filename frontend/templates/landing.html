How it works

Four steps from sign-up to profit

No broker license. No complex terminals. Just deposit, choose your move — trade or invest in shares — and withdraw when you're ready.

01
Create your account

Sign up in under two minutes. No credit card, no ID scan, no catch. Just your email, a username, and a password.

02
Deposit funds

Fund your Trovee wallet via Bitcoin or gift card. Your balance is available immediately after confirmation.

03
Trade or invest

Place short-term trades on crypto and forex assets, or buy into share plans from vetted companies and earn fixed returns.

Once your balance hits the minimum requirement, cash out to your bank, mobile money wallet, or PayPal. Most payouts process same-day.

FAQ

Common questions

If something isn't covered here, contact support from inside the app — you'll reach a human.

Is Trovee free to join?
+
Yes, completely. There is no sign-up fee, no premium tier, and no subscription. Creating your account costs you nothing — you only spend money when you choose to deposit and trade or invest.
How much can I realistically earn?
+
Earnings depend on your trading activity and share investments. Winning trades return up to 85% of your stake. Share plans earn 10–20% annually at fixed rates. Trading involves risk, and returns are never guaranteed.
When do earnings appear in my ledger?
+
Trade profits are credited to your balance the moment a trade closes. Deposits are credited after admin confirmation. Share plan returns are credited at plan maturity. There is no waiting period before you can withdraw, as long as your balance meets the minimum requirement.
Can I lose money on trades?
+
Yes. Trading involves risk. If your trade prediction is wrong, you lose the amount you staked on that trade. You can never lose more than the amount you set per trade — there is no leverage or margin on Trovee.
How long do withdrawals take?
+
Most withdrawals process within a few hours. PayPal typically arrives fastest. Bank transfers and mobile money can take up to 24 hours depending on your region.
Is this an investment platform?
+
Trovee offers both short-term trading on live market assets and fixed-return share investment plans. Trading involves real risk of loss. Share plans offer fixed returns on a defined schedule. Always only deposit and trade amounts you can afford to lose.
What countries are supported?
+
Trovee is available in 150+ countries. Your country is detected at sign-up and the correct withdrawal methods are shown automatically. If your country appears in the sign-up form, you're eligible to participate.
Can I have more than one account?
+
No. Each person may hold one Trovee account. Duplicate accounts are detected and suspended, and any pending withdrawals on those accounts will be cancelled.
